🏋️ How to Use FitFuel AI

⚡ Step 1 — Set Your Goal

Tell the AI your fitness goal in plain words or tap a quick button in the chat:

  • "I want to build muscle" → 🏋️ Muscle Gain mode
  • "I'm trying to lose fat" → 🔥 Fat Loss mode
  • "I need endurance fuel" → 🏃 Endurance mode
  • "I want to maintain" → ⚖️ Maintenance mode

🥘 Step 2 — Tell Me Your Ingredients

Even a single ingredient works — FitFuel will build a complete recipe around it:

  • "I have chicken" → single ingredient, full recipe
  • "oats" → just type the ingredient name
  • "chicken breast, broccoli, brown rice"
  • "paneer, spinach, onion, Greek yogurt"

🥗 Step 3 — Dietary Restrictions

Toggle restrictions in the chat panel or type them directly:

  • "I'm vegan" / "I'm keto" / "I'm gluten-free" / "I'm dairy-free"
  • Conflicting ingredients are automatically excluded from recipes

🎨 Step 4 — Recipe Styles

Use the Style pills to restyle any recipe instantly:

  • "make it spicy" → adds chili, pepper, cayenne
  • "make it Indian style" → turmeric, cumin, garam masala tadka
  • "make it Chinese style" → soy sauce, ginger stir-fry
  • "make it simple" → under 5 ingredients, 10 minutes
